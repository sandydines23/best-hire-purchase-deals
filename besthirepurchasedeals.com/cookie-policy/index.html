What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help sites remember your preferences and, in some cases, help site owners understand how visitors use their pages.

This site does not currently set any essential cookies. It's built as a static site with no login, shopping cart, or account system that would require them.

About Best Hire Purchase Deals

We're an independent information site dedicated to helping UK drivers understand hire purchase (HP) car finance before they commit to an agreement.

Why we built this site

Car finance is confusing, and hire purchase gets talked about far less than other finance options — even though it's one of the simplest products on the market. We started Best Hire Purchase Deals to give drivers a plain-English breakdown of how HP actually works, what it costs, and when it makes more sense than the alternatives.

We're not a lender, broker, or credit intermediary. We don't process applications and we don't give personal financial advice. What we do is research the product, explain the rules that protect you, and help you ask the right questions before you sign anything.

How we work

We research before we write

Every guide is based on FCA guidance, the Consumer Credit Act, and how HP agreements actually work in practice — not marketing copy from any one lender.

We stay independent

There are currently no live affiliate or lender partnerships on this site. If that changes, we'll disclose it clearly in the footer of every page.

We keep content current

Car finance rules and typical rates move over time, so our guides carry a last-reviewed date and get revisited as the market changes.

Editorial policy

How we research and publish our guides

This page explains how content on Best Hire Purchase Deals is researched, written, reviewed, and kept up to date.

Independence

We have no live affiliate, lender, or broker partnerships at the time of publishing. Our comparison tables use illustrative, representative figures rather than live rates from named providers, and are clearly marked as such. If we do enter a commercial partnership in future, it will be disclosed on this page and in the site footer before it affects any content.

Sourcing

Our guides are based on publicly available guidance from the Financial Conduct Authority (FCA), the Consumer Credit Act 1974, and how hire purchase agreements are structured in practice by UK lenders. Where we describe legal rights — such as voluntary termination — we base the explanation on the statutory position, not a single lender's terms.

Accuracy

Representative examples (APR, deposit, monthly payment) are for illustration only and are labelled as such throughout the site. They are not live offers, and your own rate will depend on the lender, the vehicle, and your personal circumstances. We recommend always checking the exact figures with your chosen provider before signing.

Review and updates

Each page displays a "last reviewed" date. We revisit guides periodically to check that the explanations of HP, PCP, and related products still reflect current rules and typical market conditions, and we correct errors as soon as we're made aware of them.

In plain terms

What this site is — and isn't

What we are

  • An independent, editorially-written information resource on hire purchase car finance.
  • A place to understand your rights and the questions worth asking before you apply.
  • Transparent about when figures are illustrative rather than live offers.

What we are not

  • A lender, broker, or credit intermediary — we don't process finance applications.
  • A source of personal financial advice — please speak to a qualified adviser for that.
  • Currently, a party to any live affiliate or lender partnership.

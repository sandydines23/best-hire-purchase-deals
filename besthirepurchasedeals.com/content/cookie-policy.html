Cookie policy

Last updated 17 July 2026. This page explains what cookies this site uses and why.

What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help sites remember your preferences and, in some cases, help site owners understand how visitors use their pages.

Essential cookies

This site does not currently set any essential cookies. It's built as a static site with no login, shopping cart, or account system that would require them.

Analytics cookies

If we add an analytics tool in future to understand which guides are most useful, we'll update this policy first and provide a way to opt out where required by law.

Third-party links

This site links out to other websites, including manufacturer and comparison sites referenced in our guides. We're not responsible for the cookies or privacy practices of external sites you reach from our links — check their own cookie and privacy policies when you get there.

Managing cookies in your browser

You can control and delete cookies through your browser settings at any time. Blocking all cookies may affect how some websites function, though it shouldn't affect your ability to read this site.
